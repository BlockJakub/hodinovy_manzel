Frequently Asked Questions

Do you bring your own tools and materials?

I always bring my own professional tools. Small materials (wall plugs, screws, seals) are included in the price. For larger or special parts, I can provide them or you can supply them yourself.

Which areas do you cover?

I mostly work in Prague and the surrounding area. For jobs outside this area, a small travel fee may apply.

Do you provide a guarantee for your work?

Yes, I stand by my work. If a problem arises within a reasonable time, I will come and fix it free of charge.

What jobs do you not do?

I do not perform complex electrical installations, gas work, or extensive plumbing work. These activities must be entrusted to licensed professionals. However, I can help with minor related tasks and recommend reliable specialists.
