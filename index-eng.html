Frequently Asked Questions

I usually respond within 24 hours. For urgent cases, please call me directly and I will try to arrange a same-day visit.

Cash on site is the most common method. Bank transfer is also possible if agreed in advance. For larger jobs, I may ask for a small deposit to cover materials.

Yes, I can work during weekends or evenings by prior arrangement. Please contact me in advance so we can agree on a suitable time.

I always bring my own professional tools. Small materials (screws, plugs, seals) are included. For larger or special parts, I can either buy them for you or you can provide them.

I primarily work in Prague and the surrounding area. For jobs outside this area, a small travel fee may apply.

Yes, I stand by the quality of my work. If any issue arises within a reasonable period, I will come back and fix it free of charge.

I do not perform complex electrical wiring, gas work, or major plumbing installations. For these specialized tasks, I recommend licensed professionals. However, I can often assist with small related tasks and recommend trusted experts if needed.
